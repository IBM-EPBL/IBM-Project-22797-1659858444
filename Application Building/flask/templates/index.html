What is a Phishing website?

Phishing websites are malicious sites which often appear as exact replicas of legitimate sites, but they are actually used to trick users into providing sensitive information to a third parties which may lead to unsecure attacks.

How to Check?

Is the link in that email legitimate? Whether sent by a friend or a stranger, it's unwise to click links without knowing where they take you. One of the quickest-growing security issues these days is ransomware, which is often spread by people unwittingly clicking dangerous links in emails, social networks, messengers, and other collaboration tools. Malware and phishing sites are also major risks.
